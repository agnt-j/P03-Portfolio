Poster Project

This is an individual project for IAT102, a course that teaches the basics of graphic design. The premise was to create an advertising exhibition poster for an artist or designer.

Thought Process

I chose to design a poster for Spanish designer Patricia Urquiola, who incorporates the style of minimalism in a playful matter, which suits my interest in creating simple designs.

I started off by sketching different layouts of the poster. The shapes of the placement pictures were sketched in a way for the viewer to not see the full picture, making the viewer curious to see more of the design by going to the exhibit.
